Banga soup and starch

Banga soup is a rich and hearty palm nut soup that's very popular in West Africa. Here's how to make the easy Delta version, best served with starch.

Ingredient
  • Meat

    • 1 1/2 lb Meat

  • Seafood

    • 1 tbsp Cray ground

    • 2 Fish, medium-sized dried

    • 3 Stockfish, medium

  • Produce

    • 1 Onion divided into 2 large halves

    • 2 Scotch bonnet peppers

  • Canned Goods

    • 5 cups Water or stock

  • Baking & Spices

    • 1 tbsp Banga spice

    • 1 tsp Cameroon or cayenne pepper

    • 1 Salt

    • 2 tsp Seasoning, powder

  • Nuts & Seeds

    • 1 can Palm-nut extract

  • Liquids

    • 18 tbsp Water

  • Other

    • 1-2 tablespoons of crushed obeletientien leaves or dried bitter leaves

    • 1 Oburunbebe stick

    • 250g of medium starch
